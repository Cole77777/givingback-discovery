Privacy policy

This policy describes how the GivingBack Discovery iOS app (“the App”) handles information when you use it. The App is a discovery tool for public nonprofit data; it does not require you to create an account inside the App.

Information the App processes

  • Search and browse inputs. When you type a search query or choose categories/filters, the App sends those inputs to Every.org’s Charity API to retrieve nonprofit listings and details.
  • Optional IRS verification. If you enable the “IRS Public Charity” style filter, the App may send nonprofit EINs (Employer Identification Numbers) to CharityAPI.org to check public-charity status.
  • Links and giving. When you open a donation or profile link, you leave the App for the website shown (for example Every.org) in an in-app browser (Safari View Controller). Those sites have their own privacy policies.

What we do not do (within the App)

  • The App does not include first-party user accounts, contact lists, or in-App analytics SDKs as part of this codebase.
  • The App does not sell your personal information.
  • The App does not use cross-app tracking frameworks in the App.

Third-party services

Your device contacts third parties directly:

  • Every.org — nonprofit search and metadata. See Every.org’s own documentation for how they handle requests.
  • CharityAPI.org — optional IRS-aligned checks when that feature is enabled. See CharityAPI.org’s policies for their handling of API requests.

Those providers may log standard server data (such as IP address, timestamps, and request parameters) according to their own policies.

API keys

Every.org and (optionally) CharityAPI.org credentials are stored on your device as part of the app build you install. Treat those keys like passwords.

Children’s privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through a third-party site linked from the App, contact that service directly.

Common questions

  • Search shows nothing / “connect” message. Add your Every.org public API key to the app’s Secrets.plist (or your distribution equivalent), rebuild, and try again.
  • IRS filter errors. That mode needs a separate CharityAPI.org key. Without it, use the “All” accreditation option.
  • Donations. Giving happens on Every.org (or the nonprofit’s linked site). We don’t process payments inside the App.
